Favorite Video Games Right Now

Fortnight

Fortnight is a battle royale multiplayer game developed by Epic Games. It starts out dropping 100 people onto an island with only a pickaxe. The rest is up to you to gather materials and scavenge for weapons in order to be the last person alive!

Starcraft II

Starcraft II is an RTS (real time strategy) game developed by Blizzard. You have the option to choose between three distinct races (Protoss, Terran, and Zerg) with each race offering different types of units and structures. The goal of the game is to outsmart your opponent and destroy their bases.
